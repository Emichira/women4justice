Advancing Women's Rights and Gender Equality

Women's rights are human rights. We work to ensure that women and girls can fully exercise their rights in all spheres of life through:

Legal Rights

  1. Legal literacy programs to help women understand their rights

  2. Strategic litigation challenging discriminatory laws and practices

  3. Advocacy for gender-responsive legal reforms

  4. Documentation of rights violations affecting women

Economic Rights

  1. Financial literacy and skills development

  2. Advocacy for equal pay and workplace protections

  3. Support for women entrepreneurs

  4. Challenging discriminatory inheritance and property practices

Social and Cultural Rights

  1. Combating harmful gender stereotypes and norms

  2. Promoting women's participation in decision-making

  3. Advocating for reproductive rights and health

  4. Supporting women's leadership in peace and security

Political Rights

  1. Promoting women's political participation

  2. Training for women candidates and leaders

  3. Voter education focusing on women's issues

  4. Monitoring gender-based discrimination in political processes

Program Spotlight: Women's Legal Empowerment Initiative

Our flagship program provides women with the knowledge, skills, and support to navigate legal systems and claim their rights. Through community-based paralegals, legal clinics, and rights education, we help women overcome barriers to justice.
