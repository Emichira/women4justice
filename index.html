Defending Human Dignity,

Protecting Vulnerable Communities

At Women for Justice & Change, we are dedicated to creating a world where every woman, girl, and elderly individual can live with dignity, safety, and equality. Together, we can build a future free from injustice and inequality.

01

Mission

To empower women, girls, and elderly individuals by advocating for their rights, ending all forms of violence, and promoting equality and justice for all.

02

Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of gender or age, can live free from violence, discrimination, and injustice.

03

Our Goals

  • Eradicate all forms of violence against women and girls.
  • Advocate for the human rights of women and marginalized groups.
  • Protect and promote the rights of elderly individuals.
  • Foster a culture of equality, justice, and respect for all.
About Company

Our Story

Women for Justice & Change was founded in 2023 by a group of human rights attorneys, social workers, and community activists who recognized the need for a comprehensive approach to protecting vulnerable populations. We believe in the power of collective action to drive social change and create a more just and equitable society.

Voices of Change: Stories That Inspire

At Women for Justice & Change, we are driven by the stories of those we serve. Every life touched, every voice amplified, and every right restored is a testament to the power of collective action. Here’s what some of our beneficiaries and partners have to say about the impact of our work.

"Women for Justice & Change gave me hope when I had none. After experiencing gender-based violence, I felt lost and alone. But through their counseling and legal support, I found the strength to stand up for my rights. Today, I am not just a survivor—I am an advocate for change. Thank you for helping me reclaim my life."

Jane W. - Boncahri, Kisii
