About Company

Our Story

Women for Justice & Change was founded in 2023 by a group of human rights attorneys, social workers, and community activists who recognized the need for a comprehensive approach to protecting vulnerable populations. We believe in the power of collective action to drive social change and create a more just and equitable society.

Our Approach

We believe that human rights violations are interconnected and require multifaceted solutions. Our approach combines:

  • Evidence-based interventions

    All our programs are grounded in research and best practices

  • Community-centered design

    We develop initiatives in partnership with affected communities

  • Intersectional lens

    We recognize that individuals face multiple, overlapping forms of discrimination

  • Trauma-informed care

    Our services are designed to avoid re-traumatization and promote healing

  • Sustainability focus

    We build local capacity to ensure long-term impact

Our Values

  1. Dignity:

    We respect the inherent worth of every human being

  2. Accountability:

    We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards

  3. Inclusivity:

    We embrace diversity and ensure accessibility

  4. Empowerment:

    We build capacity rather than dependency

  5. Courage:

    We stand firm in defense of human rights even when it's difficult

Our Impact

    Since our founding, we have:

  1. Provided direct support to over 500 survivors of gender-based violence through counseling and legal aid.

  2. Conducted civil human rights education workshops for community members

  3. Trained women in leadership and advocacy skills.

  4. Advocated for 3 policy changes to protect women’s and elderly rights.
